# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community for everyone.

## Expected Behavior
- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus on technical issues, not individuals.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or sharing private information.
- Repeated disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or ban users for severe/repeated violations.

## Reporting
Report conduct concerns privately through maintainers or repository contact channels.
